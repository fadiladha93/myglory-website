Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 July 2026

Effective Date: 1 July 2026

At MyGlory International Sdn Bhd ("MyGlory", "we", "our", or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website, register as a member, purchase our products, or use any of our services.

By accessing our website or using our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Identification Number / Passport Number (where required)
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Residential or Business Address
  • Date of Birth
  • Bank Account Details (for commission payments)
  • Sponsor and Placement Information
  • Username and Account Information

Business Information

  • Sales Records
  • Commission History
  • Purchase History
  • Network Structure
  • Reward and Incentive Records

Technical Information

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Device Information
  • Operating System
  • Website Usage Data
  • Cookies and Analytics Data

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Create and manage your MyGlory account.
  • Process product orders and payments.
  • Calculate bonuses, commissions, incentives, and rewards.
  • Deliver products and services.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Verify your identity.
  • Improve our products and website.
  • Send important announcements and updates.
  • Provide training, marketing materials, and business tools.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing of Information

MyGlory does not sell your personal information.

Your information may only be shared with:

  • Payment service providers
  • Courier and logistics companies
  • Technology and hosting partners
  • Government authorities where required by law
  • Professional advisors and auditors
  • Authorized MyGlory business operations when necessary

All third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical, administrative, and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Data loss
  • Misuse
  • Alteration
  • Disclosure
  • Cybersecurity threats

While no online system can guarantee absolute security, we continuously improve our security practices to safeguard your data.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website performance
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Enhance user experience
  • Support security features

Users may disable cookies through their browser settings, although some website features may become unavailable.

6. Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Update your account details.
  • Request deletion of your information where legally permitted.
  • Withdraw consent for certain processing activities.
  • Request a copy of your stored personal data.

Requests may be subject to legal and operational requirements.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Maintain business records
  • Fulfill legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

After the retention period, personal information will be securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. MyGlory is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of those websites separately.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals who are legally eligible to enter into agreements under applicable laws. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental or legal guardian consent.

MyGlory reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any revisions will become effective immediately upon publication on our website. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
